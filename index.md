# Privacy Policy for My Weekly Grid

**Last Updated:** May 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in the "My Weekly Grid" mobile application. 

### 1. Data Collection and Usage
**Personal Data:** 
My Weekly Grid does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data such as your name, email address, or phone number to any external servers. 

**Local Storage:** 
All content created by you (tasks, notes, habits, and priorities) is stored locally on your device using an encrypted Room (SQLite) database. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by the developer.

### 2. Third-Party Services
My Weekly Grid uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) to show personalized or non-personalized ads. 
*   **Consent:** You can manage your ad personalization preferences via the in-app GDPR consent dialog.
*   **Privacy Link:** [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 3. Permissions
The app requests the following permissions:
*   **Internet:** Required for loading advertisements.
*   **Post Notifications:** Required to send you reminders for your scheduled tasks (Android 13+).

### 4. Data Deletion
You have absolute control over your data. 
*   **Manual Deletion:** You can delete all your stored data at any time via **Settings > Delete My Data**.
*   **App Uninstall:** Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored database files.

### 5. Children's Privacy
My Weekly Grid does not collect any data. Since all information is stored locally, we do not have access to, nor do we store, any information about our users, including children.

### 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective immediately upon being updated in the app.

### 7. Contact Us
For any questions regarding privacy, please contact the developer via the Play Store contact information.
