Privacy Policy for My Weekly Grid

**Last Updated:** May 03, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in the "My Weekly Grid" mobile application. 

### 1. Data Collection and Usage
**Personal Data:** 
My Weekly Grid does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data such as your name, email address, or phone number to any external servers. 

**Local Storage:** 
All content created by you (tasks, notes, dates, and priorities) is stored locally on your device using a Room (SQLite) database. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by the developer.

### 2. Third-Party Services
My Weekly Grid uses the following third-party services which may collect information used to identify you:

*   **Google AdMob:** Used to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) to show personalized ads. You can manage your consent for this via the in-app GDPR consent dialog.
    *   [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 3. Permissions
The app requests the following permissions to function correctly:
*   **Internet:** Required for loading advertisements via AdMob.
*   **Post Notifications:** Required to send you local reminders for your scheduled tasks.

### 4. Data Deletion
You have full control over your data. You can delete all your stored tasks and notes at any time by going to **Settings > Delete My Data**. Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored data.

### 5. Children's Privacy
My Weekly Grid does not knowingly collect any data from children. Since all planning data is stored locally, we have no way of identifying the age of the user.

### 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
